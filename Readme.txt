OVERVIEW
--------
This was an early experiment in interactive storytelling. The player has to traverse a large area, divided into several small sections that would load whenever they walked off the screen. The objective is to reach the right end of the level.

The game can be played online at this link: http://files.myfrogbag.com/bli6du/animation19.swf

CONTROLS
--------
Arrow keys: 	Move player
Z:		Jump
